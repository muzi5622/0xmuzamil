## üöÄ Overview

This guide shows how to:

3. Transform the card into a realistic photo with AI (ChatGPT/Gemini)

## üé® 1. Make a Student Card in Canva

1. Go to [Canva Student Card Templates](https://www.canva.com/s/templates?query=student+card)  
2. Select a template  
3. Customize details:  
   - Name: Your name or fake student name  
   - School/University: Any realistic name  
   - Student ID: Random code  
   - Photo: Optional placeholder  
4. Download as **PNG** or **JPG**

---

## üì∏ 2. Transform into a Realistic Photo

1. Upload your Canva student card image to **ChatGPT (image input)** or **Gemini**  
2. Use a prompt like:  
   > "Take this digital student card and transform it into a highly realistic photo as if it was taken with a real camera. Ensure natural lighting, subtle shadows, and realistic textures. The card should look slightly worn or handled like a real physical card, with slight reflections and soft focus. Make the colors and text appear natural, as if photographed in a normal indoor environment."  
3. Save the resulting realistic image

## üß± 3. Create a Temporary Email

1. Open a temporary email service:  
   - [TempMail](https://tempumail.com/)  
   - [ETempMail](https://etempmail.com/)  

2. Copy the generated email address.  
   **Example:** `abc123@tempmail.edu.pl`

3. Keep the temporary email webpage open so you can receive the verification code.

2. Click **Get Student Offer** and agree to any policies if prompted.

3. Add a **payment method** to your Google account.  
   - Google may make a temporary verification charge (for example, **300‚Äì500 PKR**) and then refund it.

4. Upload your student card as requested.

6. Follow the remaining prompts to complete registration.
